Please use a browser that supports the "canvas" tag.
  • Download this folder with the .ply files (the models) and unzip it.
  • Choose one of the files by clicking the button "Choose File" below.
  • Click on the animation below to select it.
  • Then use these keyboard keys to play around with the object:

    • 'c': Translate your wireframe in the negative x direction
    'x': Translate your wireframe in the positive x direction
    'u': Translate your wireframe in the negative y direction
    'y': Translate your wireframe in the positive y direction
    'a': Translate your wireframe in the negative z direction
    'z': Translate your wireframe in the positive z direction
    'p': Activate/deactivate the pulse mode
    'r': Rotate
